# Privacy Policy for Pill Cycle

Effective Date: April 2, 2026
Last Updated: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pill Cycle (“we”, “our”, “the App”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and securely in accordance with Apple’s App Store Review Guidelines.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect only the information necessary to provide medication reminder and adherence features.

### 1.1 Personal Information
- Email address (used for secure login, account identification, and recovery)
- User ID (uniquely generated and managed by Firebase Authentication)

### 1.2 Medication & Health-Related Data
- Medication names (e.g., Paracetamol, Dolo)
- Dosage information (e.g., 650mg, 1 tablet)
- Medication schedules (time, frequency, and course duration)
- Medication intake status (taken, snoozed, or skipped)
- Adherence analytics and streak data
- Optional medication photos (images of your pills or prescription sheets)
⚠️ **Disclaimer:** All data is user-entered. We do not provide medical advice.

### 1.3 Caretaker Information (Optional)
- Caretaker name and relationship (e.g., family, guardian)
- Notification preferences and push notification tokens (FCM)

### 1.4 Device & App Data
- App usage events (for performance monitoring and bug fixes)
- Device OS version and App version (to ensure technical stability)

## 2. How We Use Your Data
We use your data only to operate, provide, and improve the Pill Cycle experience. Specifically:
- To schedule precise 3-ring burst notifications for your doses.
- To track adherence and notify your caretakers of missed doses.
- To securely sync your data across your devices via your account.
- **Selling Data:** We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or health data to advertisers or third parties.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
We utilize Google Firebase (a secure cloud platform) to protect your data. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections. Access to medication data is restricted by strict Firebase Security Rules—ensuring only you can access your own records.

## 4. User Rights & Data Deletion
Apple’s guidelines require that you have full control over your data.
- **Access & Edit:** You can view and modify any data in the app at any time.
- **Account Deletion:** You have the right to delete your account via the App Settings. Upon deletion, all associated personal data, medication records, analytics, and photos are permanently removed from our production systems.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
Pill Cycle is intended for use by individuals 13 and older, or by parents/guardians managing medications for children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without adult supervision and consent.
